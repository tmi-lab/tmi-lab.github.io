Projects

Our Focus

Our lab focuses on the application of machine learning to healthcare. Some specific examples include:

  • Predicting the risk of UTI in people living with Dementia.
  • Understanding the eating habits of people living with Dementia.
  • Analysing Electronic Healthcare Records (EHR) for treatment/recovery trajectroy
  • Digital consultation and digital screening using EHR data
  • Identifying the risk of neuropsychiatric symptoms in in people living with Dementia.
  • Generative AI models for neuro time-series daata analysis.

Teaching

Our lab runs a module on Machine Learning for Neuroscience, taught to MSc Computational Neuroscience students at Imperial College London. The module is taught by Professor Payam Barnaghi and is run in the Spring term. More information can be found at: https://ml4ns.github.io/
